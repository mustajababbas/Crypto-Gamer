Crypto Gamer TOKENOMIC

Crypto Gamer is the latest token in the Binance Smart Chain network, bringing together the best of all platforms in one place.
The Crypto Gamer community is a universal acupoint for our teams, players, streamers, producers, NFT creators and many others.
The Crypto-Gamer token's goal is to build a gaming platform in the Metaverse. We wish to delight people on the Metaverse at the crypto gaming platform.
Its users will be able to share and sell the content they generate, such as videos, photos, and movies, as well as sell it as games and NFTs.
